# Phone Setup

Start the container first:

```powershell
.\start.ps1
```

Then open the app from your phone on the same Wi-Fi.

Find your computer IP on Windows:

```powershell
ipconfig
```

Look for the IPv4 address, then open this on your phone:

```text
http://YOUR-COMPUTER-IP:8080
```

On Android Chrome:

```text
Menu -> Add to Home screen
```

That gives you the app icon experience immediately. The boot terminal opens inside the app.
